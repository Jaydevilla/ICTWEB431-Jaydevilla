Visions

Visions are magical crystals that grant individuals the ability to wield elemental powers. Each Vision is associated with one of the seven elements in the game:

Hydro

Hydro Visions grant control over water-based skills. Hydro abilities can freeze enemies, create water barriers, and heal allies. They are strong against Pyro enemies.

Dendro

The Dendro Vision will likely relate to nature-based abilities. Its specific effects and characters associated with it remain a mystery as of now.

Anemo

Anemo Visions allow users to harness wind energy. Anemo abilities can launch enemies into the air, create wind currents, and spread status effects. They counter Geo enemies.

Electro

Electro Visions allow users to manipulate electricity. Electro abilities can paralyze foes, trigger reactions, and deal area-of-effect damage. They excel against Hydro enemie

Cryo

Cryo Visions bestow ice-based powers. Cryo abilities can freeze opponents, reduce their movement, and create icy constructs. They are effective against Electro enemies.

Pyro

Pyro Visions empower characters with fire-based abilities. These include launching fireballs, creating fiery fields, and igniting enemies. Pyro attacks are effective against Cryo (ice) enemies.
